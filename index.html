For Mummy & Daddy 👋
Please remind your child to practise these position words often, and try to use them in everyday instructions at home. For example:
  • "Put the plate on the table."
  • "Put the cup in the cupboard."
  • "Your shoes are under the bed."
  • "Stand behind your sister."
  • "The book is next to the lamp."
Hearing and using these words every day helps your child learn faster. Thank you for practising with them! 💛
